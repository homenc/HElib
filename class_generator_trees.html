void GeneratorTrees::ComputeCubeMapping ( )

Computes permutations mapping between linear array and the cube.

If the cube dimensions (i.e., leaves of tree) are n1,n2,...,nt and N= n_j is the size of the cube, then an integer i can be represented in either the mixed base of the n_j's or in "CRT basis" relative to the leaves: Namely either i = {j<=t} i_j * {k>j} n_k, or i = i'_leaf * leaf.e mod N.

The breakPermByDim procedure expects its input in the mixed-base representation, and the maps are used to convert back and forth. Specifically, let (i'_1,...,i'_t) be the CRT representation of i in this cube, and j = {j=1}^t i'_j * {k>j} n_k, then we have map2cube[i]=j and map2array[j]=i.
